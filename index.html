Crofters: Your Digital way of Developing Agricultural resources is a bidding website that is in partnership with Food Processing Companies and supported by the Local Government Units made to help farmers to where to sell their crops at a much more reasonable price and gain the upper hand in every market transaction.

OUR MISSION:

Bridging the gaps between the market and the farmers by providing adequate alternatives in increasing their income and helping them allocate their harvest onto the market.

OUR VISION:

To open up a brighter future for our farmers using sustainable solutions and helping in our country’s agriculture continuous development.
